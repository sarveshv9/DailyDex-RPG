## The Journey Begins...

Your journey starts like any other—a quiet village, a new Pokémon, and a friendly rivalry with your neighbor, Wren. But when an unnaturally turbulent sea at the edge of the region forces you to abandon the coastal crossing and take the long way around, you begin to realize that the old local legends might be true. 

They say something ancient is sleeping beneath the waves.

As you travel through bustling gym towns, hidden settlements, and treacherous mountain passes, you'll cross paths with the enigmatic **Ashen Order**. They operate in the shadows, their presence growing stronger with every town you visit, and they seem relentlessly drawn to the towering peaks at the region's heart. 

What are they searching for in the dark caverns of the mountain? What is causing the violent tremors that put everyone on edge? 

The secrets of the legendary **Cindralith** await those brave enough to uncover them, but heed the warnings of the elders: *some things should never be awakened.*
